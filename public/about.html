About Me

Hi, I'm Daniel, a web developer and digital nomad. I'm originally from Pennsylvania, USA, but I've been living and working remotely for the past few years as I travel the world.

I used to be a civil engineer, but I realized that my true passion was in web development. I love the creativity and problem-solving challenges of building websites. I'm also passionate about helping businesses and organizations succeed online.

I'm a skilled front-end developer with experience in a variety of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP. I also have extensive experience with WordPress, and I'm proficient in all aspects of WordPress development, from creating custom themes and plugins to managing and optimizing WordPress websites. I'm also familiar with the latest WordPress security and performance best practices.

In my spare time, I enjoy hiking, trying new foods, and going on new adventures with my family. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities, so if you have a project in mind, please don't hesitate to reach out!
